What are the benefits of using aluminum tubing in manufacturing? ⌄

Aluminum tubing offers high strength-to-weight ratios, excellent corrosion resistance, and thermal conductivity, making it ideal for aerospace and automotive applications.

How is aluminum tubing produced? ⌄

It is typically produced through extrusion, where aluminum alloy is forced through a die to create specific hollow shapes and sizes.
